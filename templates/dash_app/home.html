Overview

It is crucial for decision-makers, strategists, and stakeholders to understand every aspect of their business. Having no insights on your data is a dilemma for making decisions at a broader level. Taking in account the related works, there was a need for a cumulative platform for decision making. A business intelligence web application with integrated dashboard reporting in an easy-to-use, low-cost business analytics tool is designed for business analysts, BI experts, controllers, and managers. It enables you to easily access, monitor, and report data from data source even without any SQL knowledge. Empowering everyone to dig into their data and leverage the potential of their analytics is the goal of dashboard reports. We are designing online business intelligence to apply performance management which derives actionable insights. Our system makes use of the smart analytics with various analytics option. The proposed system specializes in smart reporting to create effective strategies using an intuitive and comprehensive business dashboard. Dashboard reporting is a process used to represent key performance indicators and relevant business data in a visual and interactive way.
